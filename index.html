About me

In life, it's challenges—not avoidance—that help us grow. In the same way that I approach every challenge in my life with steady, determined progression, I love to do the same with user problems. Whether it's a piece of copy that can improve someone's life or a design that can enhance someone's experience, I see challenges as problems that can be overcome.

What I Bring to the Table

  • UX writing and simplicity: Non-sensical designs are a pain in the bum, especially for those who don't have the technological fluency. I make websites simple so you can get more sales.
  • Copywriting and branding: I've worked with some of the world's biggest brands, Nubia, ANTA, Huawei and many more. From launches, to campaigns, to email marketing, to product pages, to blog posts. Simply put, I write researched, data driven, keyword heavy copy that sells.
  • Research, research, research: Until you've walked a mile in your customers shoes you cannot understand their pains, their dreams, their nearly-theres and their setbacks. Thorough targeted research, both quantitative and qualitative is the answer. With a strong understanding of SEO, A/B testing, user interviews, highlighter testing, conversation mining and many more, my copy will guide the user through their past mistakes and toward their future successes.
  • Coding and design: Poor design turns people off. Copy can be near perfect but if a website is poorly designed then a user will not hang around for more than a minute. With a deep understanding of tools like Figma and Webflow as well as the ability to write HTML, CSS and JS, I bring a well-rounded ability that is crucial for understanding the full user experience.

Little by little, a little becomes a lot. I try to stick to this motto everyday. I love learning languages, skills, anything. I speak English and Chinese, and I'm currently learning Polish, with more skills to come. Bring it on!

Guess My Number Game

Test your luck with this simple number guessing game! Try to guess the secret number between 1 and 20.

Using event listeners and DOM manipulation, the Guess My Number game responds to user input in real-time, updating the game state accordingly.

  • Gameplay is unpredictable and fun by generating random numbers.
  • The conditional checks determine if the user's guess is correct, incorrect, or too high or low.
  • The score management system adjusts based on the user's performance, ensuring a balanced challenge for all users.

My focus was on creating a smooth and intuitive gameplay experience, demonstrating my ability to create user-centered applications using core JavaScript.

Pig Game

Challenge yourself or a friend in this classic two-player game where the first to score 100 points wins! Beware the number 1 though!

The game features a two-player turn-based system, where I used functions to handle player switches, score tracking, and win conditions.

  • Random dice roll are generated to display random outcomes which adds a level of excitement and unpredictability.
  • Responsive feedback for players through real-time DOM updates, ensuring the game remains engaging and easy to follow.
  • Score tracking and win condition logic that effectively manage game progress and determine the winner.

My approach emphasized creating a seamless and responsive gameplay experience, showcasing my proficiency in JavaScript and my ability to translate game rules into efficient, interactive code.
